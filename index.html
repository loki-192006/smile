FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things our clients ask us.

How far in advance should I book bridal makeup? +
We recommend booking your bridal makeup at least 2–3 months in advance, especially during wedding season (October–February). This ensures availability and allows time for pre-bridal sessions and trials.
Do you offer a makeup trial before the wedding day? +
Yes! We strongly recommend a bridal trial session 1–2 weeks before your wedding. This helps you visualize your look, make any changes, and ensures your big day makeup is perfect. Trials are included in our Gold and Platinum packages.
What type of makeup products do you use? +
We use only premium, internationally acclaimed brands like MAC, Kryolan, NARS, and Huda Beauty. All our products are skin-tested, long-wearing, and photography-friendly to ensure you look flawless in every photo.
Do you provide at-home bridal services? +
Yes! At-home bridal services are available in and around Thiruchendur. This is included in our Platinum Package, or can be arranged as an add-on for other packages. Please contact us for availability and pricing.
How long does the bridal makeup process take? +
A full bridal look (makeup + hair) typically takes 3–4 hours. We recommend starting early on your wedding day to allow for a relaxed, stress-free experience. Please plan accordingly when scheduling.
What are your consultation and booking procedures? +
Simply fill out our booking form, WhatsApp us, or call us directly. We'll schedule a free consultation to understand your needs, preferences, and occasion. A small advance deposit is required to confirm your booking date.
